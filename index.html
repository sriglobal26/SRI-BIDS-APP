Strategy & Pro Tips
⚡
Register Directly with Agencies
Register on SAWS, Austin Water, Houston Public Works, NTMWD, and TRA portals for direct E&I solicitation emails.
🏗️
Track Prime GC Sub-Bid Packages
Most E&I engineering work is sub'd under a GC. Monitor Matous, Eagle Contracting, RAVA, and Felix Construction for E&I design sub-packages.
💰
$1.038B TWDB HB500 Grant Wave
Texas HB500 distributes $1.038B through Aug 2027. Every recipient will need E&I engineering design — a surge of downstream bids is expected statewide.
📡
SCADA Upgrade Wave 2025–2027
Multiple Texas utilities upgrading aging SCADA due to cybersecurity mandates. Pure engineering design scope — ideal for SRI Global.
🔍
NIGP Codes for E&I Filtering
Use NIGP codes on ESBD: 913 (Electrical), 920 (Instrumentation), 956 (SCADA/Controls). Combine with keyword searches for best results.
🏘️
Harris County MUDs — High Frequency
100+ independent MUDs in Harris County each bid separately. H2bid and CivCast post new MUD E&I engineering bids weekly.
